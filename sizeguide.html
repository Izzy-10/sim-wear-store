How To Measure

Getting An Accurate Fit

📏

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape level under your arms.

📐

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, just above your belly button.

👣

Foot Length

Stand on a piece of paper, trace your foot, and measure heel to longest toe.

Still unsure? Reach out to us with your measurements and we'll recommend a size.
