Our Story

Built From Jozini, Worn Everywhere.

Sim Wear started with a simple idea: South African streetwear deserves a home online that actually gets the culture right.

🇿🇦

Sim Wear was founded by Sizwe Sibiya, a software developer student at WeThinkCode_ in Durban, who set out to build more than just an app — a real brand for the culture. What started as a class project turned into a genuine e-commerce venture bringing bold, homegrown fashion to people across South Africa.

Every piece in the catalog — from oversized tees to full outfit sets — is chosen to reflect a Sim Wear philosophy: dress different, not basic. We're building this brand the same way we build our code: intentionally, one solid piece at a time.

Today Sim Wear ships nationwide from Durban, with new drops added regularly and a growing community backing the vision.
